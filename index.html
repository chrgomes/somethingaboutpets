Compassionate, experienced animal lovers for all your pet-sitting needs.

Something About Pets dedicates itself to fulfilling your pet-sitting needs with a genuine passion for animals. Our commitment involves building lasting connections with your pets and delivering top-notch, personalized care, including dog walking, petting sitting, daily visits and in-house sitting. Rest assured, we treat your fur babies as our own, ensuring they experience the highest quality care during your absence.

Our pets grace our lives as Earth's angels, their unwavering loyalty and boundless love is a testament to the importance of dedicated care and service - a cherished responsibility for the greatest gift we've received.

Jessica Conners

Principal, Something About Pets

Why Choose Us

At Something About Pets, we believe that caring for your pets is an honor and a commitment to their well-being. Dedicated to attending to every need, showering your furry friends with love, and ensuring a joyous and healthy life for your cherished companions. Every pet deserves the best, and we're here to provide just that.
